🎁 Reverse allowance — extra free size you can give per side selling at a higher sale tonnage frees CBM → give it back as trimmed size
Benchmark 5 t/CBM. A higher sale tonnage bills fewer CBM (a discount); that freed volume can instead be given as extra size trimmed off each of the 3 faces. Per block: cm/side = ΔV·10⁶ ÷ [2·(L·W + W·H + L·H)], where ΔV = gross tonnage × (1/5 − 1/target). Uses the Specific gravity above.
